## Out of scope for Sprint 01

Specific feature screens (property detail, repair form, admin dashboards, CRM queue) are deferred to subsequent sprints. They depend on the system being in place.

## Acceptance for Sprint 01

The design sprint is complete when:

- The design canvas exists and is reachable from `.design-canvas-url`.
- Every token in `DESIGN.md` is represented as a named style in the canvas.
- Every UI primitive in the foundation list has approved variants in the canvas.
- Every variant has approved behaviour in light mode (dark mode is deferred).
- A second pair of design eyes has signed off on the foundation.
