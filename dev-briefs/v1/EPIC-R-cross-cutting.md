# EPIC-R — Cross-cutting (definition of done, glossary, open questions)

**Master spec reference:** Section R (R.1–R.4).
**Status:** CONTEXT (no implementation work).
**Paired design brief:** [design-briefs/v1/EPIC-R-cross-cutting.md](../../design-briefs/v1/EPIC-R-cross-cutting.md).

## Purpose

This brief is informational and points to the operating-system-level rules that govern *how* the work is done:

- The universal definition of done — captured in `dev-briefs/sprint-01/_cross-cutting.md` section 1.
- The domain glossary — captured in `PRODUCT.md` section 2 and in master spec Section R.2.
- The open questions — captured in master spec Section R.3 and in each epic's "Open questions" section.
- The closing acceptance criteria for the spec as a whole — master spec Section R.4.

## How other epics use this brief

- Every dev brief inherits the universal DoD without restating it. Per-brief acceptance criteria layer **on top of** the DoD.
- Every dev brief uses the canonical naming and never invents new terms for entities defined in the glossary.
- Sprint planning resolves at least one open question per sprint until the list is empty.

## Acceptance

The DoD, glossary and open-questions list are referenced — not duplicated — by every other artefact. Drift surfaces in audit.
