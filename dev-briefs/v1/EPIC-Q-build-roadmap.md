## Purpose

This brief is informational. It carries the phase plan that sprint planning maps onto. Master spec Section Q defines eight phases (core marketing, listings and search, admin, CRM, repairs, customer accounts, advanced SEO, optional follow-ons). Each phase's outcomes, features, dependencies and acceptance criteria are stated there.

## How sprints map onto phases

Sprints are typically narrower than phases. A sprint may complete one phase, or it may complete only part of one phase, or it may overlap two phases (foundation work for a later phase plus consumer work for an earlier one).

A sprint backlog `dev-todo-sprint-<n>.md` references the relevant phase. The autonomous prompt's PHASE C onwards reads the sprint backlog, not this brief directly.

## Acceptance

The phase plan in master spec Section Q is the authoritative sprint-planning input. Drift from it requires an amendment.
