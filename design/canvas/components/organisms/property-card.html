Tokens: card --radius-md, border --colour-border · image --ratio-card · hover lift translateY(-2px) + --shadow-sm over --motion-duration-fast · status fills --colour-status-* · price --text-heading-md · address --text-heading-sm · meta --text-body-sm · "added" caption --text-caption.
Accessibility: the whole card is one focusable link with a visible ring. Status badge has a verbatim aria-label (colour is never the only signal). Save button is a separate 44 px control with its own label. Sold / let / withdrawn cards replace the disabled "Book a viewing" affordance with an active "Notify me of similar" — never a dead control. Under reduced-motion, hover swaps the lift for a border-colour cue.
