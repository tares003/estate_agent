Cookie banner & consent

EPIC-N design brief — CookieBanner + granular Customise panel (PECR / PRODUCT.md §6 rule 8)
Actions
Accept all · Reject non-essential · Customise
Categories
Necessary (forced) · Analytics · Marketing · Preferences
Rules
can't dismiss without a choice · gates non-essential scripts · decision logged

Banner — first visit

Bottom-of-viewport, doesn't block content but can't be dismissed without a choice. Plain language, no legalese. Slides in over --motion-duration-base --motion-ease-emphasis. Full-width on mobile, padded inset on desktop.

Your cookie choices

We use cookies to make this site work and to understand how it's used. Choose your preferences below. Read our cookie policy ↗.

Customise panel — granular consent

Per-category toggles. Necessary is forced on and can't be switched off. Analytics / Marketing / Preferences default off until the user opts in. Non-essential scripts don't load until consent is given.

NecessaryRequired for the site to work — security, sign-in, your preferences during a visit.
Always on
AnalyticsHelps us understand how the site is used so we can improve it.
MarketingLets us show you relevant property ads on other sites.
PreferencesRemembers choices like saved searches and recently viewed.

Settled state

Once a choice is persisted the banner does not show again; a small footer link re-opens the panel.

Tokens: banner --colour-surface-base + --shadow-md, --radius-lg · toggle on --colour-brand-primary · z --z-overlay-mid · slide-in --motion-duration-base --motion-ease-emphasis.
Accessibility: all three banner actions are in tab order and keyboard-reachable. Each category toggle is a real checkbox with an aria-label; "Necessary" is conveyed as forced-on in text, not just visually. The decision is logged with a timestamp (PRODUCT.md §6 rule 8).
