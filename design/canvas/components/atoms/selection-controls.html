Selection controls

EPIC-L design brief — Atoms · Checkbox · Radio · Switch
Controls
Checkbox · Radio · Switch
States
unchecked · checked · focus · disabled · indeterminate
Rules
real inputs under visual layer; 44 px hit area

Checkbox

Whole row is the hit target (≥ 44 px). The visual box sits over a real <input type="checkbox">.

Radio

Single choice from a set. Grouped under one name in a role="radiogroup" fieldset.

I'm looking to

Switch

Immediate on/off for settings — e.g. enforcing 2FA, toggling a listing live. Uses a real checkbox underneath with role="switch".

Tokens: control fill --colour-brand-primary · radius --radius-sm (checkbox) / --radius-circle (radio, switch knob) · focus --shadow-focus · transition --motion-duration-fast --motion-ease-standard · disabled --colour-surface-sunken.
Accessibility: all three are real inputs visually hidden but focusable; the label wraps the control so the whole row toggles and the hit area is ≥ 44 px. Focus ring uses --colour-focus-ring. Switch carries role="switch"; radio groups use a <fieldset> + <legend>.
