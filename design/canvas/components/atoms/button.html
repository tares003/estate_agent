Tokens: height --size-button-{sm|md|lg} · radius --radius-md · padding --space-4/5/6 · fill --colour-brand-primary / --colour-danger · hover --colour-brand-primary-hover · transition --motion-duration-fast --motion-ease-standard · focus --shadow-focus (--colour-focus-ring).
Accessibility: every variant is a real <button>, keyboard-focusable with a visible ring (--colour-focus-ring). Loading buttons set aria-busy="true" and are non-interactive. Disabled buttons use disabled (not pointer-events alone). Hit area is ≥ 44 px in both dimensions on touch. No hover-only affordance — every state is reachable by keyboard and touch.
