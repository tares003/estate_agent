§2a.3 pattern — pack-gated attribute group

In a multi-tab form (e.g. the property editor), attribute groups that depend on a pack appear as a + Add [Pack-name] attributes secondary CTA. Activating it opens the pack-enable modal; after enabling, the group appears in place at the next form load (a toast confirms — no reload needed in-session).
