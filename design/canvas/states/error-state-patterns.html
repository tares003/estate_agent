Error state patterns

design-requirements.md §7 — calm, honest, recoverable (PRODUCT.md §7 "calm in crisis")
Levels
field-level · form-level · section-level · full-page (404 / 500)
Voice
acknowledge, don't blame; say what to do next; never lose the user's input
Rule
errors announced via live region; associated with their field

Field & form level

The smallest, most common errors. A field error is associated via aria-describedby; the message is specific and tells the user how to fix it.

Enter a complete email address, like name@example.co.uk.
We couldn't send your enquiry

Something went wrong our end — your message wasn't lost. Please try again in a moment.

Section & full-page

When a whole region or page fails to load. Always offer a retry and a route out — never strand the user.

This section didn't load

We had trouble loading the property map. The rest of the page is fine.

We can't find that page

The link may be old or the property may have been removed. Let's get you back on track.

Accessibility: field errors use aria-invalid + aria-describedby; banners use role="alert" (assertive). The 404 keeps the global header/footer so navigation never disappears. Retry and exit are real focusable buttons/links.
