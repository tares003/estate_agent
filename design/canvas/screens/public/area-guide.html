Living in Didsbury

Leafy, village-feel and well-connected — one of South Manchester's most loved neighbourhoods.

About the area

Didsbury blends a genuine village atmosphere with easy access to the city. Independent cafés, characterful pubs and green spaces along the Mersey sit alongside Edwardian and Victorian homes. It's a long-standing favourite with families and professionals for its schools, its high street and its quick Metrolink hop into town.
