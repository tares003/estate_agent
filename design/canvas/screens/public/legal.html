Privacy policy

Last updated 14 May 2026 · [Agency to confirm with their solicitor]

Who we are

Estate Agent Ltd ("we", "us") is the data controller for the personal information you provide through this website and our branches. We're registered in England & Wales (no. 00000000) and a member of the Property Ombudsman.

What we collect

Depending on how you use our services, we may collect:

  • Contact details — name, email, phone, address.
  • Enquiry details — the properties and services you're interested in.
  • Transaction records — viewings, offers, tenancies and repairs.
  • Technical data — how you use the site (with your cookie consent).

How we use it

We use your information to respond to enquiries, arrange viewings and valuations, manage tenancies and repairs, meet our legal obligations, and — only with your consent — to send you property alerts and market news.

Legal basis

We process your data on the basis of contract (to provide the services you ask for), legal obligation (for example anti-money-laundering checks), legitimate interests (running our business responsibly), and consent (for marketing, which you can withdraw at any time).

Who we share with

We share data only where necessary — for example with contractors assigned to a repair, deposit-protection schemes, or referencing providers — and always under appropriate agreements. We never sell your data.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or erase your personal data, to object to or restrict its processing, and to data portability. To exercise any of these, contact us using the details below. You also have the right to complain to the ICO.

Contact & complaints

Email privacy@estateagent.co.uk or write to our Didsbury branch. If you're unhappy with how we've handled your data, you can contact the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.
