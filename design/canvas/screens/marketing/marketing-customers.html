Customer story

How Northern Homes cut response time by 60%.

A nine-branch agency moved off a patchwork of tools onto one modular platform — and never looked back.

Northern Homes had grown to nine branches across the North West, but its systems hadn't kept up — a website from one vendor, a CRM from another, and lettings run largely on spreadsheets. Leads slipped through the gaps, and no one had a single view of the pipeline.

The switch

They started on the Professional tier for the website, listings and CRM, then switched on the Lettings and Feedback packs as they migrated each branch. Because pricing is modular, they only paid for what they'd actually moved across — no big-bang migration, no double-running costs.

"For the first time we can see every lead, every branch, in one place — and the team actually trusts it."— Operations Director, Northern Homes

The result

Within a quarter, average enquiry response time fell by 60%, and the Feedback pack lifted their review volume fivefold. Assignment rules meant valuations reached the right branch manager instantly, and the tenant portal cut inbound repair calls dramatically.

They're now rolling out the Insights pack to forecast pipeline across all nine branches from a single dashboard.
