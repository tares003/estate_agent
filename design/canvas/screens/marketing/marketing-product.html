How it works

Up and running in three steps.

Start with Core, add Lettings, New Homes or Feedback. Your plan, your pace — change it any time.

Bring your brand

Your domain, your colours, your logo. The site and emails carry your identity, not ours.

Go live

Import your listings, route your leads, and switch on the public site — usually within a week.

Automated review requests, moderation and an agent league table.

Cross-branch reporting and pipeline forecasting from one dashboard.
