Modal

Centered dialog over a scrim. Shown here in its open state. In: scale 0.96 → 1 + opacity over --motion-duration-base --motion-ease-standard; out reverses with --motion-ease-exit.

Contact agent

Send a message about Palatine Road, Didsbury. The branch usually replies within one working day.

Focus management: on open, focus moves into the dialog and is trapped (Tab cycles within). Escape closes; focus restores to the trigger. The dialog has role="dialog" aria-modal="true" and is labelled by its title.

Drawer

Side panel for filters (desktop) and menus. Slides from the edge over --motion-duration-base. On mobile, the advanced-filters drawer opens from the bottom instead.
