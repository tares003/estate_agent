## Purpose

Informational. Sets visual direction for every subsequent design brief.

## What this epic establishes for design

- The platform's seven surfaces — designer must understand the shape of each before designing a single screen.
- The aesthetic direction in master spec Section M.1: bold-editorial, photography-first, dark accent + warm neutral, editorial typography, sticky utility at edges, unmissable conversion buttons, mobile-first.
- The "one property card, used everywhere" principle — designers must not multiply card patterns.
- The "no carousel that auto-advances" principle.

## Surfaces affected

All — this brief is referenced by every other design brief.
