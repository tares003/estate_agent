# EPIC-P — Technology requirements (design)

**Dev brief:** [dev-briefs/v1/EPIC-P-technology-requirements.md](../../dev-briefs/v1/EPIC-P-technology-requirements.md).
**Master spec reference:** Section P.
**Status:** CONTEXT (no standalone visual surface).

## Purpose

Informational. Technology capabilities have no visual surface but the chosen products may have visual implications (a mapping product determines marker styles; an SMS product determines templated content limits; an analytics product determines a banner row of vendor logos in the privacy policy).

When the stack is committed in `CLAUDE.md`, designers review whether any chosen product imposes a visual constraint and update the relevant per-component design entries.

## Open design questions

None at this level until the stack is chosen.
