## Purpose

Informational. Cross-cutting design rules — the universal patterns that every other design brief inherits.

## Universal design rules

- Every interactive component shows its disabled, hover, focus, active and loading variants.
- Every data-fetching surface shows its loading, empty, error and success states.
- Every form shows inline field errors and a top-of-form summary when more than three errors are present.
- Every primary CTA is visually distinct from secondary actions.
- Every destructive action is colour-coded with the danger token and confirms before executing.
- Every modal traps focus and is dismissible via Escape.
- Every page-level title becomes the document title and the breadcrumb leaf.
- Every page has a meaningful empty state — never a blank canvas.
